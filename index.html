CodeHigh
2017
http://codehigh.net/
CodeHigh is a platform for coders to compete real-time. After 2 to 8 players join, a random algorithm problem is given. Each player writes his/her own solution within 3 minutes. Once everyone submits (or gives up), they vote for the most creative solution. CodeHigh lets users learn from others while having fun.
